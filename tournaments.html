Official Tournament Rules

  1. Your prize is your XP. Every XP you earn at a tournament table is yours to keep — that is the challenge and the reward.
  2. The bankroll is on us. Each participant receives $5,000 in virtual tokens provided by BaccaElite.com, valid only at the tournament table. The tokens are not a prize and are not added to your balance.
  3. Four tables per day, every 6 hours. Tables open at 12:00 AM, 6:00 AM, 12:00 PM and 6:00 PM (site time), so players in every time zone get their chance. Only these 4 tables run per 24 hours.
  4. Six seats per table. When a table fills up, entry closes for that table.
  5. One tournament per player per day. You may join only one of the four daily tables — everyone gets a fair shot at the prizes.
  6. The winner is the player with the most XP at their table. If two players finish tied, the one who reached that XP total first wins.
  7. Winners are published. Each table's winner appears above with their name, XP won and date, and stays published on this page for 7 days.
  8. Practice your strategy. Tournaments are the perfect chance to test your method and maximize XP gains without touching your own balance — take advantage of it.
